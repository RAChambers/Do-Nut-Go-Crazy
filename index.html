Fred the Baker Baking Company

Fred the Baker has been in business baking delicious donuts for 25 years! He is ready to retire but somehow wants his love for donuts continue on. Fred has created Donuts Amok! so that everyone can virtually bake donuts forever!

×

A-HA!

Donuts Amok! was inspired and modeled after a game created by a French programmer in 2013 called Cookie Clicker. By clicking on the donut, users can virtually bake a single donut per click. They can then use those donuts to buy purchase Auto Clickers & Click Multipliers which can create/bake even more donuts!!
